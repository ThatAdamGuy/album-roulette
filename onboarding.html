🎲 Album Roulette

Play a random album from your YouTube Music library — start to finish, the way albums were meant to be heard.

🎲 Fully random

Click the 🎲 in the YouTube Music header (or this extension's toolbar icon, from any tab). A random album from your library starts playing at track 1 — no shuffle, no radio, just the album. It avoids repeating your last 20 picks.

Semi-🎲 & genres

Click the ▾ next to the die (or right-click the die) to pick by genre — Jazz, Soundtracks & Musicals, Classical, and more — or hit Semi-🎲 “Deal me 6” to get six albums from six different genres and choose by mood.

Genres take a little while to appear. On first run, the extension quietly identifies each album's genre in the background using free public music databases (Apple's iTunes Search and MusicBrainz). A large library takes an hour or so — the popover shows progress, and it only happens once. The 🎲 itself works immediately.

Private by design

No account, no API key, no server. Your library list stays in your browser; the only thing ever sent anywhere is album title + artist to the public genre databases above.
